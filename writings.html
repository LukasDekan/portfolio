War of Finance Essay

This essay was based on a passage from Middlemarch by George Eliot, highlighting a married couple’s financial conflict. The first draft lacked structure and clarity.

After receiving teacher notes, I rewrote the essay to better organize my points and emphasize theme over summary. I made sure each paragraph had a clear purpose and stronger evidence.

Through revision, I learned to be more concise and analytical. The final version is more refined and thoughtful, showing how feedback and revision are essential to improving my writing.
