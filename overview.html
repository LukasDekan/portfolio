Portfolio Overview

In my portfolio, you’ll find several pages, each dedicated to a unique project or skill:

The Thanksgiving Website page showcases one of my favorite web development projects, inspired by the warm aesthetics of Fall. This project reflects my eye for seasonal design and front-end creativity.

On the Playdate and Pulp page, you’ll explore my experience using a unique game development tool designed for handheld gaming. It highlights my ability to adapt to new platforms and think outside the box in game creation.

The Completed Tutorials page features the foundational tutorials that helped me build my programming skills and confidence. These tutorials represent my early steps into the world of coding and problem-solving.

The Landscape Painting page presents a detailed and atmospheric scene of a castle, forest, and mountains, showcasing my interest in natural beauty, composition, and creating a sense of depth through painting.

For art lovers, the Art page combines my passion for pixel art and concept art. You’ll see how I create tiny sprites for games, sketch out ideas in my sketchbook, and experiment with visual storytelling across styles and mediums.

The Writings page includes two pieces of analytical writing: The War of Finance, which explores personal budgeting and financial challenges, and The Blinding Torch, an essay focused on the theme of human pride. These essays reflect my ability to analyze and express complex ideas through writing.
